The *Tyrannosaurus* is the most **fierce** dinosaur that ever existed.

The T-Rex lived in the [Late Cretaceous Period](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Late_Cretaceous).

## Features

- Big teeth
- Small arms
- Thick tail
