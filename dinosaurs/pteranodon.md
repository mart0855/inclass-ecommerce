The *Pteranodon* is an **amazing** flying dinosaur.

The Pteranodon lived in the [Late Cretaceous Period](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Late_Cretaceous).

## Features

- Toothless beak
- Expansive wings
- Pointed crest
